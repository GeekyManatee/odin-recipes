Homemade Carnitas

Description

Carnitas are a fatty, lightly crispy pork dish hailing from Mexico.

The pork is slowly braised until fork tender and then crisped up under a broiler

Ingredients

  • Pork shoulder
  • Oranges
  • Cinnamon
  • Onion
  • Chicken Broth
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Cut fat off pork
  2. Sear pork in dutch oven
  3. Add aromatics
  4. Add chicken broth
  5. Braise in 375 oven for 5 hours
  6. Remove from oven and pull apart pork before setting on baking dish and broiling for 10 minutes
