Mud Soup

Description

Mud soup is an ancient, earthy delicacy passed down through childhoods for millennia.

It uses fresh ingredients straight from your garden and be prepared entirely with your hands!

Ingredients

  • Mud
  • More mud
  • Even more mud
  • So much god dang mud
  • If you think you have enough mud, you don't
  • Some rocks too, I guess

Steps

  1. Get all that mud
  2. Put it in a bucket
  3. Grab a spoon and eat it
  4. Throw the rocks at people who tell you to not eat mud soup
