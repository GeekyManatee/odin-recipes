Dragon Tail

Description

Fortunate for us, dragon tails regenerate overtime.

Unfortunate for dragons is their perfectly marbled tails are delicious.

Ingredients

  • Armor
  • Sword
  • Shield
  • Willpower
  • Cast iron skillet

Steps

  1. Put on your armor
  2. Pick up your sword
  3. Equip your shield
  4. Gather up all the willpower you can muster
  5. Cut off the dragon's tail
  6. Cook the slab over an open fire in your cast iron skillet
